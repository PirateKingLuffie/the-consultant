Rashi Ratna - Gemstones

Explore the sacred gemstones of Vedic astrology.
Each stone is unique in its power and purpose.

Blue Sapphire

Blue Sapphire (Neelam)

Planet: Saturn (Shani)
Benefits: Career growth, discipline, focus, and protection from negativity.
Who should wear: Those under Sade Sati or seeking stability and prosperity.

Yellow Sapphire

Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj)

Planet: Jupiter (Guru)
Benefits: Wisdom, prosperity, marriage, and spiritual growth.
Who should wear: Those seeking knowledge, wealth, or good fortune in marriage.

Ruby

Ruby (Manik)

Planet: Sun (Surya)
Benefits: Leadership, confidence, vitality, and fame.
Who should wear: Those needing motivation, recognition, or a boost in self-esteem.
