Rashi Ratna - Gemstones

Discover the power of Vedic gemstones. Each Rashi Ratna holds unique energies to enhance your destiny, health, and fortune.

Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj)

Planet: Jupiter (Guru)
Benefits: Wisdom, prosperity, marriage, and spiritual growth.
Who should wear: Those seeking knowledge, wealth, or good fortune in marriage.

Blue Sapphire (Neelam)

Planet: Saturn (Shani)
Benefits: Discipline, focus, career success, and protection from evil.
Who should wear: People facing obstacles, seeking stability, or under Sade Sati.

Ruby (Manik)

Planet: Sun (Surya)
Benefits: Leadership, confidence, vitality, and fame.
Who should wear: Those needing motivation, recognition, or a boost in self-esteem.

Pearl (Moti)

Planet: Moon (Chandra)
Benefits: Emotional balance, peace, mental clarity, and maternal happiness.
Who should wear: People with anxiety, mood swings, or seeking calmness.

Diamond (Heera)

Planet: Venus (Shukra)
Benefits: Luxury, charm, creativity, and relationship harmony.
Who should wear: Artists, lovers, or those wanting to enhance beauty and relationships.

Coral (Moonga)

Planet: Mars (Mangal)
Benefits: Courage, health, energy, and victory over enemies.
Who should wear: People lacking confidence or facing health issues.

Cat's Eye (Lehsunia)

Planet: Ketu
Benefits: Protection from evil, spiritual awakening, and sudden gains.
Who should wear: Those undergoing Ketu Mahadasha or seeking mystical growth.

Emerald (Panna)

Planet: Mercury (Budh)
Benefits: Intelligence, communication, business success, and healing.
Who should wear: Students, businesspeople, or those needing clarity in speech.

Hessonite (Gomed)

Planet: Rahu
Benefits: Success in legal matters, clarity, and overcoming confusion.
Who should wear: Those affected by Rahu dosha or facing delays and obstacles.
