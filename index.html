WHY ASTROLOGY?

The Divine Science

Astrology reveals the will of the Gods. It is a predictive science with its own methods, claims and findings that have forever inspired and allowed people with insights into different aspects of their life.

The Nine Planets (Navagrahas)

In Vedic astrology, we study 9 cosmic bodies that influence human lives:

Sun (Surya)
Moon (Chandra)
Mars (Mangala)
Mercury (Budha)
Jupiter (Brihaspati)
Venus (Shukra)
Saturn (Shani)
Rahu (North Node)
Ketu (South Node)

Each planet has unique influences - some bring positivity while others like Rahu and Ketu can bring challenges depending on their position in your Kundli.

The 12 Houses

Your Kundli contains 12 houses, each representing different aspects of life:

  • 1st House (Ascendant): Self, personality, physical traits
  • 5th House (Putra Bhava): Creativity, romance, pleasure
  • 7th House: Marriage and partnerships
  • 10th House: Career and social status

The placement of planets in these houses determines their influence on your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is astrology so accurate?

Astrology is based on scientific principles established thousands of years ago. Our astrologers study planetary movements and their influences on human life with precision.

What can astrology predict?

Astrology can provide insights about career, relationships, health, finances, and more by analyzing planetary positions and their interactions.

Can astrology predictions change?

Predictions may change based on your actions (karma), incorrect birth data, or astrological remedies that alter planetary influences.

How can I benefit from astrology?

Astrology helps you understand life challenges, optimal timings for important decisions, and remedies to overcome obstacles.
